1. Introduction

Voice Diary ("we," "our," or "the App") is a personal AI-powered voice journaling application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By using Voice Diary, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

What we collect: Email address, name (from Google Sign-In)
How we collect it: When you sign in with Google
Why we need it: To create and manage your account, authenticate you, and associate your diary entries with your profile
How long we keep it: Until you delete your account

2.2 Audio Data

What we collect: Voice recordings during conversations with Aria (your AI companion)
How we collect it: When you press the voice button and speak in the app
Why we need it: To process your speech, generate AI responses, and extract meaningful insights
How it's processed:
  • Transmitted securely to Google's Gemini AI service via Firebase
  • Transcribed and analyzed to generate responses
  • Not permanently stored - audio is processed and discarded
How long we keep it: Temporary processing only (typically seconds to minutes)

2.3 Diary Content (User Content)

What we collect: Transcriptions of your voice conversations, extracted memories, reflections, and insights
How we collect it: Created from your voice conversations with Aria
Why we need it: To provide you with:
  • A searchable diary of your conversations
  • Personalized AI responses based on your history
  • Weekly reflection insights
  • Progress tracking and goal reminders
How it's stored:
  • Encrypted in Google Cloud Vertex AI Memory Bank
  • Associated with your unique user ID
  • Only accessible through your authenticated account
How long we keep it: Until you delete it or delete your account

2.4 Usage Data

What we collect: Basic app usage statistics (conversation frequency, error logs)
Why we need it: To improve app performance and fix bugs
How long we keep it: Up to 90 days

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Enable voice conversations with Aria, store your diary entries, and generate weekly reflections
  • Personalization: Remember your goals, progress, and conversation history to provide contextual responses
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and enhance features
  • Security: Authenticate users and prevent unauthorized access to accounts
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and support requests

We do NOT use your data for:

  • ✗ Advertising or marketing
  • ✗ Selling or sharing with third parties for their purposes
  • ✗ Training AI models beyond your personal experience
  • ✗ Tracking you across other apps or websites

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with Google Sign-In
  • Access Controls: Data is only accessible through authenticated API requests
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on Google Cloud Platform with enterprise-grade security

Note: While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You use Voice Diary at your own risk.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:
