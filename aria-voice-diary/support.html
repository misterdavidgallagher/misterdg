Welcome to Aria Voice Diary

Aria is your personal AI companion for voice journaling. Simply talk about your day, thoughts, and feelings, and Aria will help you reflect, track your goals, and gain meaningful insights over time.

Getting Started

1

Sign In

Open the app and sign in with your Google account. This keeps your diary entries secure and private to you.

2

Allow Microphone Access

When prompted, allow microphone access. This is required for voice conversations with Aria.

3

Start Talking

Tap the microphone button and start speaking. Talk about your day, how you're feeling, your goals, or anything on your mind.

4

Let Aria Respond

Aria will listen, respond thoughtfully, and help you reflect. She remembers your previous conversations to provide personalized support.

Key Features

Voice Conversations

Have natural voice conversations with Aria. She's designed to be a thoughtful listener who helps you process your thoughts and emotions.

Diary Entries

Your conversations are automatically transcribed and saved as diary entries. Browse your past entries anytime to see your journey.

Weekly Reflections

Each week, Aria generates personalized insights based on your conversations, helping you identify patterns and track progress toward your goals.

Memory & Context

Aria remembers what you've shared - your goals, challenges, and wins. This allows for deeper, more meaningful conversations over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your diary entries are encrypted and only accessible through your authenticated account. We never sell your data or use it for advertising. Audio is processed temporarily and not stored permanently. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual diary entries within the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below.

Why isn't Aria responding?

Check that you have a stable internet connection. Aria requires an internet connection to process your voice and generate responses. Also ensure microphone permissions are enabled in your device settings.

Can I use Aria offline?

No, Aria requires an internet connection to function. Voice processing and AI responses are handled by cloud services to provide the best experience.

How do I view my past diary entries?

Tap on the diary/entries icon in the app navigation. You'll see a chronological list of all your past conversations and can tap any entry to view the full transcript.

What should I talk about with Aria?

Anything you'd write in a personal journal! Common topics include: how your day went, emotions you're processing, goals you're working toward, challenges you're facing, wins you want to celebrate, or just thoughts you want to get out of your head.

Does Aria replace therapy or professional help?

No. Aria is a journaling tool designed for self-reflection, not a substitute for professional mental health support. If you're struggling, please reach out to a qualified mental health professional.

How do weekly reflections work?

Based on your conversations throughout the week, Aria generates insights highlighting themes, progress on goals, and patterns she's noticed. These appear automatically and help you see the bigger picture of your journey.

Tips for Best Experience

Speak clearly - While Aria handles natural speech well, speaking clearly helps with accurate transcription.
Be consistent - Regular check-ins (even short ones) help Aria provide better insights and track your progress over time.
Be honest - Aria is a judgment-free space. The more authentic you are, the more valuable your reflections will be.

Troubleshooting

Microphone not working?
Go to Settings > Privacy > Microphone and ensure Aria Voice Diary is enabled.
App crashing or freezing?
Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, ensure you have the latest version installed.
Sign-in issues?
Ensure you're using the same Google account you originally signed up with. Try signing out and back in if you experience issues.
